What is 3R system

    Supports 3R (Reduce-Reuse-Recycle) Green Concept!
    Understand the 3R Concept (Reduce-Reuse-Recycle)
    Basically, the 3R (Reduce-Reuse-Recycle) Concept is a sequence of steps on how to manage waste properly.
    The top priority is Reduce, which is to reduce waste generation, then Reuse, and then Recycle, to give waste material a second chance before disposing them to the landfill.
Reduce – reduce waste generation from the start by bringing your own shopping bags, using products that can be used repeatedly, and so on

Reuse – reuse materials that can and are safe to be reused, one of them is by making handicrafts or through the upcycling process

Recycle – recycling waste by melting, chopping to be re-formed into new products that most likely to experience a decline in quality
